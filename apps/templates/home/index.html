Why is this important?

At CivicCit we believe civic engagement increases emotional intelligence and motivates individuals towards conscientious community action We aim to have individuals work on their ability to collaborate across diverse perspectives and positively impact communities

Our 5 Features

Our application provides five features which include: a civic events system, a civic quiz system, a scheduling system, a progress report system, and a civic information hub system.
